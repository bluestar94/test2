Welcome To Interstate Farm Company

Interstate Farm ltd is based and registered in Tanzania since November 2016 As an agro-business company. Inter State Farm provides comprehensive business solutions to smallholder coffee-farmers in Tanzania by ensuring that they use modern production methods and support them with farming inputs so that they increase coffee returns and ensure the best quality of coffee.The company specifically aim at ensuring there is fair value along whole coffee supply chain from the farm to the final coffee consumer whereby it also supports the smallholder coffee farmers to seek for reliable markets of their coffee produce.
